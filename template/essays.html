On accessibility, sustainability, diversity, and inclusion

Accessibility in software looks different than it would relative to accessibility in the outside world but still considers similar disabilities that people may have. Disabilities come in many forms, and roughly 15% of the world’s population, almost 1 billion people, experience some form of accessibility needs. Accessibility in tech is relatively new but there are many ways to make things that are not only accessible to those who need it, but beneficial to all users.
